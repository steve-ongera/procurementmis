How to Create a Procurement Plan

  1. Select Budget Year: Choose the financial year for this procurement plan
  2. Select Department: Choose your department (only one plan allowed per department/year)
  3. Enter Title: Provide a clear, descriptive title for the plan
  4. Add Description: Optionally add more details about the plan
  5. Create: Click "Create Plan" to proceed to adding items
Important: After creating the plan, you'll need to:
  • Add procurement items with estimated costs and quarters
  • Assign budget allocations to each item
  • Submit the plan for approval
