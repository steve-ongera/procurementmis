Amendment Guidelines

When to Request Amendments:

  • Add Item: New requirement emerged after plan approval
  • Remove Item: Item no longer needed or requirements changed
  • Modify Item: Changes to quantity, specifications, or cost estimates
  • Budget Change: Reallocation of budget between items
  • Quarter Change: Timeline adjustments for procurement
  • Method Change: Change in procurement approach

Justification Requirements:

  • Clearly explain the reason for the amendment
  • Describe the impact if amendment is not approved
  • Provide any relevant supporting documentation
  • Ensure alignment with department objectives
