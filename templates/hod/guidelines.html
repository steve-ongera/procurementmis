Requisition Process Flow

1

Create Requisition

Staff member creates a requisition with detailed specifications and justification

2

HOD Review & Approval

Head of Department reviews and approves/rejects within 3 working days

3

Budget Verification

Finance verifies budget availability and compliance

Procurement Processing

Procurement office processes according to thresholds

5

Purchase Order

PO issued to selected supplier and delivery tracked

Best Practices for HODs

Timely Review

Review and process requisitions within 3 working days to avoid delays

Budget Awareness

Always verify budget availability before approving requisitions

Clear Documentation

Ensure requisitions have proper justification and specifications

Feedback

Provide clear comments when rejecting to help staff improve

Policy Compliance

Ensure all requisitions comply with university procurement policies

Monitor Trends

Regularly review department spending patterns and budget utilization
