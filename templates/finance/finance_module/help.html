Budget Management

Creating a Budget

  1. Navigate to Finance → Budgets → Create Budget
  2. Select the budget year, department, and category
  3. Enter the allocated amount
  4. Add description and reference number if applicable
  5. Click "Create Budget" to save

Budget Tracking

Monitor budget utilization through the Budget Tracking page:

  • View department-wise and category-wise breakdowns
  • Track allocated, committed, and spent amounts
  • Monitor utilization percentages
  • Identify over-utilized or under-utilized budgets

Budget Reallocation

Transfer funds between budget lines:

  1. Go to Budget Tracking → Reallocate Budget
  2. Select source and destination budgets
  3. Enter amount and justification
  4. Submit for processing

Invoice Verification

3-Way Matching Process

Verify that invoices match purchase orders and goods received notes:

  1. Review pending invoices from the dashboard
  2. Open invoice for verification
  3. Compare invoice with PO and GRN:
    • Verify supplier matches
    • Check quantities received vs invoiced
    • Confirm unit prices match PO
    • Validate total amounts
  4. Note any discrepancies
  5. Approve as "Matched" or mark as "Disputed"

Approving for Payment

After verification, approve invoices for payment:

  1. Review matched invoice details
  2. Verify supplier bank details
  3. Add approval comments if needed
  4. Click "Approve for Payment"

Payment Processing

Creating a Payment

  1. From approved invoice, click "Process Payment"
  2. Verify invoice and supplier details
  3. Enter payment information:
    • Payment date
    • Payment amount
    • Payment method (Bank Transfer, Cheque, EFT, Mobile Money)
    • Payment reference/transaction number
    • Bank details (for cheques)
  4. Add notes if necessary
  5. Click "Create Payment"

Approving Payments

Review and approve pending payments:

  1. Navigate to pending payments
  2. Review payment details
  3. Verify supplier bank information
  4. Approve or reject with comments

Payment Schedule

View and manage upcoming payments by due date to ensure timely processing.

Reports & Analytics

Available Reports

  • Expenditure Report: Track spending by department and supplier
  • Budget vs Actual: Compare budget allocations with actual spending
  • Payment History: Review all completed payments
  • Transaction Report: Detailed transaction listing

Exporting Reports

Most reports can be exported to CSV format:

  1. Open the desired report
  2. Apply filters as needed
  3. Click "Export" button
  4. Choose format (CSV, PDF, Excel)
  5. Download file

Analytics Dashboard

View comprehensive financial analytics including:

  • Monthly spending trends
  • Category-wise expenditure
  • Department-wise spending
  • Top suppliers by value
  • Payment method distribution
